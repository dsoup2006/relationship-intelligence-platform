# Nexus 3.0 Vision

## Mission

Nexus is a Relationship Intelligence Platform that helps users organize, understand, visualize, and discover relationships between people, organizations, places, events, assets, and information.

Rather than storing isolated notes, Nexus builds a living knowledge graph where everything is connected.

---

## Philosophy

Everything is an Entity.

Everything is Connected.

Everything has a History.

Everything should be Explainable.

---

Nexus should feel like:
